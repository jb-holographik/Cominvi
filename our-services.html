Our Process

At CoMinVi, underground mining is a coordinated, multi-phase operation built on precision, safety, and efficiency. Every project follows a rigorous sequence that ensures structural stability, high productivity, and a strong focus on safety.

Drilling

01

This is the process of creating boreholes using percussive rotary motion on the rock mass, which are later charged with explosives for detonation.

Blasting

02

The controlled use of explosives to fracture or fragment the rock mass

Mucking

03

The process of cleaning and extracting blasted material from tunnels, stopes, or other works, and depositing it in loading bays, bins, or trucks using a scooptram.

Haulage

04

The transportation of blasted material—either ore or waste rock—using dump trucks or low-profile trucks, which are loaded with a scooptram

Ground Support

05

A technique used to ensure ground stability, using anchoring systems, mesh, shotcrete, or steel frames as needed to secure fractured, pressurized, or collapse-prone rock.

Supervision
